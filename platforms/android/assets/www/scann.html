BarcodeScanner

BarcodeScanner plug-in provided by Phonegap Build, the inspiration for the part was taken from a tutorial of an old issue of Web Designer magazine I found at home. Since the planning I wanted to apply a third party plug-in to understand how the mechanics work as I'm interested in the interactivity between an electronic device such a smart phone and other types of media such as a printed paper, this app is useful to an old idea where the magazine would publish QR codes that grants access to special vouches which will be used to promote business partners who advert on the magazine pages.
